Data sources
Bustl POS
Daily sales, orders, AOV
Manual entry
Enter daily Bustl figures in Enter Figures. To automate: export daily CSV from Bustl → email to Make.com email parser.
Shopify
Online orders & revenue
Manual entry
Connect via Make.com in ~20 min. Native Shopify connector available. See guide →
Xero
Bank, bills, GST, expenses
Manual entry
Xero has a native Make.com connector. OAuth — just log in when prompted. Pulls bank balance and invoices.
iPayroll
Wages & payroll totals
Manual entry
iPayroll sends email summaries. Make.com watches your email and parses the wage total automatically each pay day.

Sign up at make.com

Connect Shopify (20 min)
New Scenario → Shopify module → Watch Orders → authorise with your store URL and API key (Shopify Admin → Apps → API). Set to run daily at 8pm.

Connect Xero (15 min)
Add Xero module → Get Bank Transactions + List Invoices. OAuth connects by logging in to Xero.

iPayroll via email
Make Email module → Watch Emails → filter for iPayroll → Text Parser extracts wage total. Auto-fills on pay day.

Bustl via CSV email
